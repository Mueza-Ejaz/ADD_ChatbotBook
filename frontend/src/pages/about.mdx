## About the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

Welcome to the companion website for the "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics" textbook. This book serves as a comprehensive guide to the exciting and rapidly evolving fields of physical artificial intelligence and humanoid robotics.

### Our Mission

Our mission is to demystify the complex concepts behind intelligent robots, providing readers with both theoretical knowledge and practical insights. We aim to inspire the next generation of engineers, researchers, and enthusiasts to contribute to the advancement of AI in physical forms.

### What You'll Learn

*   **Fundamentals of Physical AI:** Explore the core principles that enable AI to interact with the physical world.
*   **Humanoid Robotics:** Delve into the design, control, and applications of humanoid robots.
*   **Perception and Sensing:** Understand how robots perceive their environment using various sensors.
*   **Manipulation and Locomotion:** Learn about the mechanics and control algorithms for robot movement and interaction.
*   **Ethical Considerations:** Discuss the important ethical implications surrounding the development and deployment of advanced robotics.

### For Students and Professionals

Whether you are a student beginning your journey in robotics, a researcher looking for a comprehensive reference, or a professional seeking to expand your knowledge, this textbook offers valuable insights and up-to-date information.
