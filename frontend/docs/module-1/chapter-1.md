# Chapter 1: Introduction to Physical AI

This is the introductory text for Chapter 1. Physical AI is an exciting field combining AI and robotics.

## Section 1.1: What is Physical AI?

Physical AI refers to intelligent systems that interact with the physical world. This includes robots, autonomous vehicles, and smart devices.

### Subsection 1.1.1: Key Concepts

Key concepts include embodiment, situatedness, and interaction. These concepts are fundamental to understanding how AI systems operate in physical environments.
